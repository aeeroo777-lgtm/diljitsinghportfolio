The Artist Behind

I'm Diljit Singh — a video editor and colorist driven by a passion for turning raw footage into compelling visual narratives.

Every edit I craft is a deliberate conversation between story and emotion. Whether it's a cinematic short film, a brand commercial, a music video, or a YouTube creation, my goal remains the same: to captivate audiences and deliver content that hits different.

Armed with DaVinci Resolve, Premiere Pro, and After Effects, I specialize in color grading and seamless editing — bringing every frame to life with cinematic precision.
